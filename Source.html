Morton High School Gaming

Sponsored by Mrs. Quintanilla and Mr. Kuck

MHS Gaming is the place to go after school to hang out with friends and play some awesome games! Gaming club is a collaborative group effort between multiple gaming groups, and features two affiliated yet individual clubs at the moment:

Video Game Club

Video Game Club a.k.a. VGC, and formerly known as Gaming Club, involves hanging out with fellow gamers of all skill levels and playing our favorite video games.

Meetings for Video Game Club are held in Room #140, every Friday from 2:30 pm to 4:30 pm.

If you want to bring in games or controllers for the consoles that VGC will have for the week, go ahead! Or maybe you want to bring in your own console; in that case, make sure it is okay by asking our club sponsor Miss Quintanilla first.
Handhelds are always allowed and encouraged.

Trading Card Game Club

Trading Card Game Club, Collectible Card Game Club, or just Card Game Club (TCGC!) is a club centered around playing and teaching newcomers how to play our favorite trading card games.

Meetings for Trading Card Game Club are also held in Room #140, every Friday from 2:30 pm to 4:30 pm.

TCGC! usually features Yu-Gi-Oh! and Magic: The Gathering for reference on what you might want to bring with you.
